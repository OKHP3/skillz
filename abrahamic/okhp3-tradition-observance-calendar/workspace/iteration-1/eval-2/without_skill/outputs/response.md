In 2027, the dates for Easter are as follows:

- **Western (Gregorian) Easter:** March 28, 2027
- **Orthodox (Julian/Paschal) Easter:** May 2, 2027

Western Easter falls on the first Sunday after the Paschal Full Moon (which occurs on Sunday, March 21, 2027). Since the full moon falls on a Sunday, Easter is observed on the following Sunday, March 28. Orthodox Easter calculations, which use the Julian calendar and a different lunar cycle calculation, result in May 2 for that year.
