# Final Check

- Confirm the source platform, source artifact, and requested output.
- Confirm the destination type, page/database/data-source URL or ID, and visibility.
- Fetch the target page or schema before writing.
- Confirm the write mode: preview, report-only, create, update, or upsert.
- Confirm page content and child content will be preserved as intended.
- Confirm database properties and relation targets came from the fetched schema.
- Confirm source-level and extract-level duplicate checks were completed.
- Confirm privacy, redaction, and authorization boundaries.
- Fetch the result after writing when possible.
- Report created, updated, skipped, redacted, failed, and pending items.
